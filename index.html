    FAQ:

    1. What is baruch CEO?

    The College Entrepreneurial Organization (CEO) is a student organization that is dedicated to promoting, cultivating, and nurturing the entrepreneurial spirit that is unique to Baruch students. We want to prioritize that everyone is welcomed and encouraged to pursue their dreams, ventures, and most importantly establish lifelong friendships. Our judgment free zone will serve as a creative and applicable extension of the current undergraduate curriculum and provide undergraduate students with the support necessary for achieving their entrepreneurial aspirations. CEO strongly believes that by attending discussion panels, workshops, networking events, and many other activities Baruch students will gain the confidence and direction they need to launch innovative and successful ventures.
